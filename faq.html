Am I protected loss or damage while my goods are in transit?
Yes, but it depends on the protection plan you choose. In general You have pre-selected Full-Value Protection or Released Value Liability(60 cents per pound per article);
Full-Value Protection
Under this protection plan, if any article is lost, destroyed or damaged while under United Van Lines' interstate authority, it will either 1) repair the article to the extent necessary to restore it to the same condition as when it was received by United or pay you for the cost of such repairs; or 2) replace the article with an article of like kind and quality or pay you for the cost of such a replacement. An additional charge applies for this option and is included upfront in every estimate. United will determine the appropriate settlement method to be used. United's total liability for loss or damage will not exceed the amount you declare as the value of your shipment. However, the minimum total declared value must be at least equal to the weight of your shipment multiplied by $6.00 per pound.
Released Value Liability (basic protection)
With this type of valuation, United's maximum liability for loss or damage to any article in the shipment is 60 cents multiplied by the weight of the article. This is the basic liability level and is provided at no charge.
